## 5. Legs/Torso assembly

- Preparation: 5 min
- Assembly: 5-10 min


#### Requirement:
![](../img/poppy_humanoid_assembly_BOM.jpg)

**Sub-assemblies**
- Legs
- Torso

**Robotis parts:**
- 16x Bolts M2.5x4

**Cables:**
- 1x 3P 140mm

**Motor configuration:**
- 1x Alimentation 12V
- 1x SMPS2Dynamixel
- 1x USB2Dynamixel or USB2AX
- A computer...
